Lasagna

Description

Everyone loves a good lasagna, right? It's a great way to feed a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck. It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days.

It is one of my favourite Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato
  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Pork
  • Tomato Paste
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Some kind of cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta sheets in salted water.
  2. Cook sauce
  3. Assemble pasta in a deep dish. Start layering by placing a sheet at the bottom of the pan.
  4. Pour a layer of sauce over the pasta and spread evenly. Then add a layer of cheese. Repeat steps until pasta/sauce/space runs out.
  5. Pour more cheese on top and place in broiler to get a nice crispy crust of melted cheese.
